Gaze Books, LLC  Author Contract  02/03/2024

I allow gaze to print copies of my book on my behalf. 
I allow gaze to sell and distribute such copies. 
I can revoke these allowances at any time. 
Gaze will pay me 50% of the net profit of each book of mine it sells.
Net profit = cover price - costs.
Costs = paper + ink + shipping + tax + Stripe fee (for online sales) + discounts (for sales to retailers and distributors) + traveling (for sales at book fairs).
